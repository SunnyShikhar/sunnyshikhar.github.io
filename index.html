About Me

I am a Management Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I [consider myself] a social butterfly with an insatiable thirst for knowledge. I wholeheartedly love learning, whether it is a new language, software or musical instrument, which is why I am particularly fascinated by the Tech industry as it is constantly evolving.

I [also enjoy socializing with others]. I look for any opportunities to engage in [producive] interactions with people, such as attending Leadership or Tech Conferences. I recently organized and hosted the Canadian Institute of Industrial Engineers (IIE) 2016 Student Conference along with my fellow Team Leaders.

The following graphic summarizes my main hobbies, passions and life motto.

Data

"What gets measured, gets managed" is my life motto. I'm able to perform at my highest level by collecting and analyzing data from every aspect of my life like health, finance, sleep etc.


Technology

I am fascinated by technology. Social media has allowed me to communicate with friends and family across the world while wearable tech has improved my daily lifestyle.

Leadership

Being an active leader in the community has always been important to me. From being President of Student Council in high school to Social Team Lead for IIE 2016 Conference.


Music

Music is my sanctuary, whether it is listening or playing music. It is my source of inspiration and relaxation. Making music challenges my imagination and helps me grow creatively.

Games

Board games bring my family together while video games allow me to play with friends from anywhere in the world. Video games were my first inspiration to learn programming.

Sports

Staying active is my favourite way to rejuvenate. Basketball, swimming and badminton are a few sports I play often. I also avidly follow NBA and it's athletes. Go Raptors!
